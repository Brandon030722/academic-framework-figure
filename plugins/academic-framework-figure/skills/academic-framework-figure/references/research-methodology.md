# Evidence-corpus protocol

The public corpus contains metadata and coded observations only. It does not redistribute paper figures.

Sampling unit: 15 venue-year groups, four main-track papers per group. Candidate positions are approximately 10%, 40%, 70%, and 90% of the official proceedings list. A candidate is replaced by the nearest eligible main-track paper when it lacks a principal method figure within the first four pages or belongs to a workshop/demo/dataset-only track.

For every retained paper, inspect the abstract, the paragraph introducing the method, the main figure, and its caption. Record the source URL, PDF URL, figure/page, layout, branch/panel counts, raster-image share, palette size, typography class, icon density, connector routing, train/inference encoding, shared-parameter encoding, and assigned profile.

When an official PDF is blocked, retain the official acceptance/proceedings URL and inspect an author-hosted or arXiv version of the same manuscript. Search snippets are discovery aids, not acceptance evidence.
