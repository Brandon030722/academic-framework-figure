# Copyright and provenance

Do not copy or publish conference paper figures, screenshots, logos, or photographs unless the user holds the necessary rights. Citation does not grant redistribution rights.

Safe public artifacts include bibliographic metadata, categorical measurements, short factual summaries, original icons, original templates, and newly drawn abstract examples. When reconstructing a user's figure privately, preserve a provenance note for every raster asset and keep it out of the public plugin unless redistribution is authorized.
